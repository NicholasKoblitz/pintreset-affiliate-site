---

## Intro
Color sets the mood. This guide explains undertones, balance, and practical testing so you can pick a palette with confidence.

---

## Key Rules
- **Undertone awareness:** warm vs cool undertones matter more than you think.  
- **60/30/10 rule:** 60% dominant, 30% secondary, 10% accent.  
- **Test in the room:** paint test swatches and observe at different times.

---

## Room-by-room Tips
- **Living rooms:** neutrals + one warm accent.  
- **Bedrooms:** calming pastel or muted tones.  
- **Kitchens:** crisp neutrals or high-contrast cabinets with neutral walls.
